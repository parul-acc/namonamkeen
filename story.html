The Legacy

Indore's Flavor,
Delivered with Love

It all started in the heart of Madhya Pradesh. Indore isn't just a city; it's the food capital of India, where the aroma of *Sev* and *Jeeravan* fills the streets every morning.

At Namo Namkeen, we wanted to bottle that authentic taste. What began as a small kitchen experiment by Parul Gangwal has now grown into a family of thousands of snack lovers.

Why "Namo"?

Namo represents devotion—devotion to purity, taste, and tradition. We don't use machines to taste our food; we use our hearts. Every batch is tasted by our family before it reaches yours.

"We don't sell products that we wouldn't feed to our own children. That is our only quality standard."

- The Founders

Authentic Indian Sweets & Snacks, delivered to your doorstep. The Royal Taste of Indore.
