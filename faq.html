Help & FAQs

Do you deliver all over India?
No! Currently we deliver we delivery only in Indore and Mhow.

Absolutely. We operate a 100% vegetarian kitchen using only high-quality plant-based ingredients.
What is the shelf life of the products?
Most sealed packs stay fresh for 45 day to 60 days. Once opened, we recommend storing them in an airtight container.
Do you accept Cash on Delivery?
Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) for local orders in Indore/Mhow.
